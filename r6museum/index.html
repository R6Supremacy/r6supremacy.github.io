What is R6?

R6 is a Roblox rig type, consisting of six parts: head, torso, right arm, left arm, right leg, and left leg. It was the first rig type added to Roblox and, for a long time, it was the only one available, which is why it holds nostalgic value for many people. R6 features a blocky, simplistic body with basic animations. It serves as the staple and caricature of Roblox, used frequently in both newer and older games. Contrary to what many people say about R6 being an outdated rig type that no longer sees use, it is still widely utilized in both frequently updated and inactive games, as well as by both older and newer accounts. Many people still have a fondness for it. Its size cannot be changed, and it maintains a specific height. It does not have animations, emotes, or layered clothing, which can be seen as advantages.
